Boudien De Vries' Electoraat en Elite (then and now)

In 1986 Boudien de Vries published her book on the elite of Amsterdam. To describe her data she used maps providing aggregated neighbourhood level data.

Today we are able to:

  • provide the same data on a non-aggregate level
  • provide the data on original (ca. 1860) maps of Amsterdam
  • provide the result in an interactive way (click on parts of map)
  • provide the query directly for you to edit (and double check the results!)
