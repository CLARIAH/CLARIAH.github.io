GRLC meets Triply's Data Stories

Once upon a time there were SPARQL queries. Brilliant to retrieve data with, horrible to share over the web. Basil eased this bitter taste somewhat, but it was only GRLC that allowed SPARQL queries to taste like API's. Online executable queries, awesome... complete with tags, and a summary and provenance... But great food is not just about taste... it's also about presentation....

Enter Data Stories.. and as it so happens... perfectly compatible with GRLC. For example, the query below actually resides here, stored with GRLC tags and all.
